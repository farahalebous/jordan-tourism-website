Petra & Wadi Rum Adventure

Experience two of Jordan's most iconic destinations in one unforgettable journey. Begin with a full day exploring the ancient Nabatean city of Petra, walking through the narrow Siq to discover the magnificent Treasury and other archaeological wonders.

Continue to Wadi Rum, where you'll enjoy a 4x4 jeep tour through the stunning red desert landscapes, followed by an authentic Bedouin dinner and overnight stay in a traditional camp under the starry desert sky.

Jerash & Amman City Tour

Explore the best-preserved Roman city outside of Italy at Jerash, where you'll walk through colonnaded streets, visit ancient temples, and witness the impressive Oval Plaza and Hadrian's Arch.

Continue to Amman, Jordan's vibrant capital city, where you'll visit the Citadel with its ancient ruins, the Roman Theatre, and explore the bustling downtown souks. Experience the perfect blend of ancient history and modern Jordanian culture.
