About Jordan

The Kingdom of Jordan

Jordan, officially the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan, is a country rich in history and culture, located at the crossroads of Asia, Africa, and Europe. With a history spanning over 10,000 years, Jordan is home to some of the world's most significant archaeological sites and natural wonders.

From the ancient Nabatean city of Petra, one of the New Seven Wonders of the World, to the therapeutic waters of the Dead Sea, the lowest point on Earth, Jordan offers travelers an unparalleled journey through time. The country's diverse landscapes include the dramatic red desert of Wadi Rum, the fertile Jordan Valley, and the ancient Roman ruins of Jerash.
